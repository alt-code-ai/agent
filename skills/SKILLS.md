### presuavive-reading-and-writing

> Create a new skill presuavive-reading-and-writing that provides expert guidance on reading and writing of persuasive texts,
focusing on practical elements of formal argument (for example, premisis, forms of reasoning, cogency, fallacies) 
and also elements of style.  Synthesise techniques from well A Practcal Study of Argument by Trudy Govier, 
The Elements of Style by Strunk and White and other well regarded sources in both argment and style.  Use the skill-creator skill, and
use the exa mcp to search for extra context.  Build additional skill context as required for stronger
guidance.  For reading and critique, the skill should provide deep guidance to identify strengths and weakness of the text, identifying fallacies and
other failures of cogency, and offer counter-arguments. For writing the skill should provide deep guidance on improving the cogency of the text, identifying and
eliminating fallacies, logical inconsistencies and other well understood and documemented errors.  The skill shold include a gloassary or library of well known 
fallacies, indexed by their techincal and common (lay) names.

### technical-writing

use the exa mcp to search for extra context.  Build additional skill context as required for stronger
